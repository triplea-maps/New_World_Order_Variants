================= RULES ====================

Rules v2 apply.

Technology Mechanized Infantry is active, this means
that Landtransport and Halftrack transport units.
Halftrack only Inf+Art.
LandTransport only Inf+Art.
Movement of transport doesn't stop with unloading.

Amphibious assaults only from LandingBoat.

---(1)------------- Construction Rules -----------------

Factory is placed normal in place turn, Bunker is placed
in place turn with edit mode. Bunker purchase
and placement is not limited by Factory limit, its additional.
Bunkers can be placed only in own territories owned
at the start of the turn equivalent to Factory placement.

---(2)--------------- Neutral Parties Rules -----------------

This 2 rulesets can be combined.

THIS RULESET IS OPTIONAL TO SELECT:
RULE 1.)
Option (Movement By Territory Restricted) checked.
Neutrals move only in their own territory.
RULE 2.)
Option (Movement By Territory Restricted) unchecked.
Neutrals move also outside their own territory.

THIS RULESET IS OPTIONAL TO SELECT:
RULE 1.)
Neutrals are set to AI control.
RULE 2.)
Neutrals are splitted.
Default to Neutrals not part of alliances and restricted to own territories. Hold 11 capitals to win.
AXIS: Spain, Turkey, Mideast
ALLIES: Skandinavia, Polish, Yugoslavia, Greece

============== CONNECTIONS ================

Bosperus: Istanbul to Ankara no land connection, only by sea.

Warsaw - Lithuania
Konigsberg - South Belarus
From Dover to Belgium, Lille direct connection ONLY for Airunits, to enable SBR British-Germans.
